## STRATEGY 2: The 6 Pin Types That Convert

You need to rotate these 6 pin formats. Each serves a different search intent:

### 1. The "Save This" Exercise Demo Pin
- **Format:** Vertical image (1000×1500) showing one exercise pose
- **Text overlay:** Exercise name + "Hold for [time]" + "Menopause Belly Fat"
- **What links to:** Your `/free-guide` page
- **Example:** Picture of a wall sit with text "Wall Sit: 30 Seconds = Flat Belly 🔥"

**Content bucket (15 pins):** One pin per isometric exercise + 3 combo pins

### 2. The Infographic Pin
- **Format:** Full infographic as a tall pin (1000×2100)
- **Text overlay:** "The 34 Changes of Menopause Explained" or "Your Menopause Timeline by Age"
- **What links to:** Your `/free-guide` or `/start` page
- **Example:** The timeline + symptom cards from your infographic as a scrollable pin

**Content bucket:** Your infographic can be split into 4-5 separate infographic pins

### 3. The "Did You Know?" Myth-Buster Pin
- **Format:** Bold text on pastel background + small icon
- **Text:** "Hot flashes aren't 'all in your head' — here's what's actually happening"
- **What links to:** Your `/free-guide` or `/start` page
- **Example:** Fact vs myth cards

**Content bucket (10 pins):** One per symptom with the "Why it happens" explanation

### 4. The Routine/Checklist Pin
- **Format:** Step-by-step list with checkboxes
- **Text:** "Your 5-Minute Morning Menopause Routine" or "Doctor Visit Checklist"
- **What links to:** Your `/doctor-prep` or `/breathing` page
- **Example:** "4-7-8 Breathing: 1 Minute to Calm a Hot Flash"

**Content bucket (5 pins):** Doctor prep checklist, morning routine, bedtime wind-down, 4-week program overview

### 5. The Quote / Affirmation Pin
- **Format:** Beautiful text on gradient background
- **Text:** "You are not broken. You are in transition." or "Menopause isn't the end — it's a system update."
- **What links to:** Your app homepage or `/start`
- **Example:** Inspirational quotes with soft floral backgrounds

**Content bucket (10 pins):** Pull quotes from your infographic and articles

### 6. The Testimonial Pin
- **Format:** Quote card with name + age + result
- **Text:** "I lost 3 inches off my waist in 8 weeks doing isometrics — Sarah, 52"
- **What links to:** App store or `/start`
- **Example:** Use the testimonial format from your existing ad assets

**Content bucket (5 pins):** Each with a different result focus

## STRATEGY 5: Viral Growth Hacks (Free)

1. **Pin to trending topics:** When "menopause" trends (October is Menopause Awareness Month), double your pinning

2. **Join group boards:** Search Pinterest for "menopause group board" or "women's health group board" — request to join. Each group board gives your pins exposure to that board's followers

3. **Collaborate with accounts in your niche:** Comment on 3 pins/day from other menopause/wellness accounts. They'll often repin your content

4. **Idea Pins** (Pinterest's version of Stories): Create short 30-second video pins of you explaining one exercise or one symptom — these get prioritized by the algorithm. No fancy equipment needed — just you talking to your phone camera

5. **Pin your infographic in pieces:** Your big infographic is gold. Split it into 5 tall pins (Timeline, Symptoms, Relief Methods, Quick Facts, Data Charts). Each one links back to your `/infographic` page
