Your Menopause Timeline

What to expect at every age — and what actually helps

🌱 35–45

Early Perimenopause

Cycles shift — shorter, longer, heavier. PMS worsens. Sleep starts fraying. Most women don't realize this is perimenopause yet.

🎢 45–51

Late Perimenopause

Intense phase. Periods skip. Hot flashes, night sweats, brain fog, anxiety, rage episodes. This is when most women seek help.

🔄 51+

Menopause

12 months with no period. Symptoms may still be intense but begin to stabilise. Bone density and metabolism become the focus.

🌿 55+

Post-Menopause

Hormones settle. Hot flashes often ease. Joint health, vaginal changes, weight management, and bone protection are key.
