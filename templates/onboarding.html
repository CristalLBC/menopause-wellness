💗 Why This App Exists

For generations, menopause was whispered about in secret — if it was spoken of at all. Our mothers didn't know what was happening. Our grandmothers suffered silently. We were told it was "just a phase" and to "push through it."

We're breaking that silence.

"I was 47 when the hot flashes started. I thought I was dying. My doctor said it was anxiety. It took me two years and three different doctors to learn it was perimenopause. Nobody should have to fight that hard to understand their own body."

Menopause affects every single woman who lives long enough to reach it. And yet most of us enter it completely blind — not knowing what symptoms are normal, what helps, or even what to ask our doctors.

This app is the guide we all deserved. Every feature was built to answer a real question a real woman asked — "Why am I waking up at 3 AM?" "Why is my belly growing?" "Why do I feel so alone?"

You are not broken. You are not alone. And now you have a place to start.

🗺️ Your App — A Quick TourHere's what each section does and how to use it

🏋️

Exercises

15 isometric exercises designed specifically for menopausal bodies — no jumping, no equipment, no joint strain. Each exercise includes instructions, modifications, and target hold times.

💡 Start with the Wall Sit or Front Plank. Hold for 15 seconds and work up.
🎵

Brainwave Tones

13 isochronic tones that use sound frequencies to guide your brain into specific states — deep sleep, calm focus, hot flash relief, energy boost. Use with headphones for best results.

💡 Try "Hot Flash Calm" when a flash starts, or "Deep Sleep" 15 minutes before bed.
🔭

Symptom Decoder

Select your symptoms and the Decoder tells you what stage you're likely in, which exercises target those exact symptoms, which tones help, and what articles explain it all.

💡 Select 3+ symptoms for the most accurate results. Try it first!
📋

Doctor Visit Prep

Rate your symptoms, enter lab results, and pick questions to ask. Then print or save a one-page summary to hand your doctor. You'll get better answers when they see everything in one place.

💡 Fill this out before every appointment. It saves 10 minutes of history-taking.
🌬️

Guided Breathing

Four breathing patterns (Wim Hof, 4-7-8, Box Breathing, Calming Wave) with an animated circle that expands and contracts — so you breathe WITH the app. Includes a 60-second "Emergency Calm" for hot flash moments.

💡 Use 4-7-8 during a hot flash. Use Wim Hof in the morning for energy.
🌙

Sleep Log

Track bedtime, wake time, hot flash interruptions, alcohol, and exercise each morning. After 5 entries, the app reveals patterns — like "you sleep 1.5 hours worse on nights with alcohol."

💡 Log every morning. The insights get smarter with more data.
🌸

Perimenopause Hub

A dedicated landing page for the most confusing phase. Self-assessment checklist, curated exercises and tones, plus links to every tool that helps during perimenopause specifically.

💡 Start here if you're 35-51 and not sure what's happening.
💗

Pelvic Floor & Sexual Health

Everything you need to know about pelvic floor changes at menopause — Kegels done correctly, intimacy guides, lubricant advice, and conversation starters for your partner.

💡 This is the most under-addressed topic. Share it with a friend.
📈

Infographic Appendix

The full interactive menopause guide — timeline, 34 body changes explained, hormone charts, and a personalized relief plan builder. Everything in one beautiful page.

💡 A great place to send a friend who's just starting her research.
📰

Weekly Research Briefing

Every Monday, the app scans PubMed for the latest menopause research and delivers a plain-language summary of the most interesting studies — so you stay informed without reading medical journals.

💡 Appears in your chat every Monday morning.
📅

More Tools

Symptom Tracker, Mood Tracker, Journal, Community Forum, 4-Week Program, and Workout Timer — built to support every part of your journey. The sidebar navigates you to everything.

💡 The sidebar (left side of the screen) is your home base. Everything is one click away.
