Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account on Menopause Wellness, we collect your email address and a username. You may optionally provide your age range and menopause stage to personalize your experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Personalize exercise and tone recommendations
  • Track your progress across sessions
  • Send occasional updates (only with your consent)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use Gumroad for payment processing. Gumroad handles all payment data — we do not store credit card information. Payments are processed through Gumroad's secure PCI-compliant infrastructure.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. You can also update or correct your personal information through your profile settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address associated with this application.
