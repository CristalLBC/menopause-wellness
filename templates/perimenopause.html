📅 The Two Faces of Perimenopause
🌱
Early Perimenopause
Typically ages 35–45
Cycles start shifting — shorter, longer, heavier, lighter. You might notice PMS getting worse, occasional hot flashes, mood changes around your cycle, and sleep starting to fray. Fertility declines but is still possible. Many women don't even realize this is perimenopause yet.
Irregular periodsWorse PMSMood shiftsEarly sleep issues
🎢
Late Perimenopause
Typically ages 45–51
This is the intense phase. Periods are 60+ days apart or skipping entirely. Hot flashes and night sweats become frequent and intense. Brain fog, anxiety, rage episodes, and crushing fatigue are common. This is when most women finally realize something is wrong and seek help.
Skipped periodsIntense hot flashesBrain fogPanic/anxietyJoint pain
